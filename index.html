About London Heathrow Airport

Heathrow is the major gateway to the United Kingdom and London. This is one of the top ten busiest airports in the world. This Airport is handling an average of 1300 flights to 194 destinations around the globe and 206,800 passengers every day and the first choice for the business traveller visiting London. The largest airport in the UK has located 17 miles away from the City of London connected with the London underground and rail network. But taxi is the most popular way of travelling to/from Heathrow. With 360 Airport Transfer, it takes an average of 55 minutes during pick hours and 40 minutes during off-pick hours to travel from London city centre to Heathrow.
